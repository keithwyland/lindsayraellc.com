Therapeutic Massage

Schedule Therapeutic Massage

Therapeutic massage treatment is customized to the client’s needs to help restore function to injured "soft tissues" (muscles, tendons, and ligaments). Performed on the table, this therapy may involve the use of various types of massage with a lubricant (lotion, crème, gel, or oil) and modest draping. Goals will be discussed prior to beginning the session, and time will be planned appropriately.

Pre- & Post-natal Massage

Pre- and post-natal massage shares many of the goals of regular massage — to relax tense muscles, ease sore spots, improve circulation and mobility, and just make you feel good – but it is specifically tailored to the unique needs of pregnant women or new moms and their changing bodies. Massage is performed in a side-lying position utilizing a body support system.

* A Doctor’s note is required for all prenatal clients. Please contact Lindsay Rae, LLC to schedule an appointment.

45 min: $55
60 min: $70
